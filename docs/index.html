What we do

 

The KAIST IAM Group develops and applies Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs) to explore realistic net-zero pathways. Our work combines environmental science, energy systems engineering, economics, and public policy to simulate scenarios & strategies for climate-resilient development.  

 
   
     
🧩
     

Model building

     

Advancing IAMs and energy-system modules tailored to Korea and global contexts.

   
   
     
📊
     

Policy analysis

     

Evaluating mitigation options, technology deployment, and just transition routes.

   
   
     
🤝
     

Open collaboration

     

Working with universities, institutes, and government to co-produce actionable insights.
