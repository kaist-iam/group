Graduate

Fully Funded PhD/M.S. Position in National Decarbonization Pathways

The KAIST IAM Group is seeking highly motivated and talented students to join our fully funded research project on national decarbonization pathways. This position offers the opportunity to contribute to cutting-edge research and collaborate with global experts.

The successful candidate will be responsible for developing robust decarbonization pathways for Korea using the GCAM-KAIST model and working with international partners to create global climate neutrality pathways. Background in sustainability or climate change, experience with optimization or simulation modeling, and computational skills using R or Python are ideal.

Apply → hmcjeon@kaist.ac.kr

Postdoctoral

Postdoctoral Researcher in Next-Generation Integrated Assessment Model Development

The KAIST IAM Group and Meta-Earth Research Center seek highly motivated PhD graduates to join our fully funded research project focused on next-generation integrated assessment model (IAM) development. This position offers the opportunity to carry out innovative research, apply AI in modeling, and collaborate with global experts.

The successful candidate will help design and develop IAMs using the latest AI techniques, working alongside domain experts to simulate sustainable futures. Ideal qualifications include a background in sustainability or climate change, experience with AI tools or simulation modeling, and strong programming skills in Python or R.

Apply → hmcjeon@kaist.ac.kr
