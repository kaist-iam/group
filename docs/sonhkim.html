Son H. Kim

 

 
   

Son H. Kim

   

Visiting Professor    

   

Graduate School of Green Growth and Sustainability, KAIST    

 

Dr. Son H. Kim is a Visiting Professor at KAIST GGGS and is on leave from the Joint Global Change Research Institute at Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL).

As a Senior Earth Scientist at PNNL, he has over 30 years of experience in developing and applying integrated assessment models that bridge human and Earth systems. He is the creator of the modeling architecture of the widely used Global Change Analysis Model (GCAM).

Dr. Kim holds a Ph.D. in Nuclear Engineering from MIT and focuses on analyzing energy systems and climate change using integrated assessment models.

Research Interests

  • Integrated Assessment Modeling
  • Energy Systems
  • Climate Change
  • Model Development
